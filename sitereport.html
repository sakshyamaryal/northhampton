MY experience in Learning Web Development

As i am always curious student i was fond of computers and how its mechanism works since my childhood. AS a field of interest i choose to learn computing for my future. I have thought to make my passion my profession.

It has been really good experience for me learning web development because i have experience of coding c-programming for 1 years. This web development module has made my base of coding strong and made my ability to type code faster. I learnt that nothing is easy to achieve as i have been trying hard fulfilling the requirements of the assignment.

Ups and downs

It was difficult to understand the basic of web development and i made many mistakes. Still i didnot have good sense of designing. For me HTML was not so hard but Css made designing tough for me but now i think that it is more easy to design every website using CSS rather than using HTML only. I am still struggling to do Hamburger icon and i am still trying my best to make my webpage beautiful.

This has to be one of the best module for me as this module is making everyone's coding and designing bases strong.

My Module Experience

At first it was difficult for me to understand the web development. After i learned basic of web development and i cleared my doubt with my module tutor and he is really helpful towards all the student i attended every extra classes possible. Now i am feeling comfortable to do my task.

Development Of Site.

Technical and design.

I was really satisfied when our tutor taught about linear gradient and i thought i will make use of linear gradient in my website. The mixture and variations of the colors made my website attractive. I took ideas from 99design.com and w3 school helped me a lot doing this assignment.

I developed this site by giving my best i searched best design possible for my website. Talking about technical i did not use any things that we are not able to do. This is done by my own i didnot use any software that autogenerate the codes.

IN Conclusion

I am really enjoying doing my given task and also i am glad i choosed NAMI. I have learned and prepared myself for the given assignment. This given task has given me opportunity to test my skill, creativeness and my preparation.

I see vision of professionalism as i complete my course from University OF Northampton. The given opportunities to learn at University of Northampton makes me more strong and go beyond boundries.
